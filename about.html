About the Project

This platform was created to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in pharmaceutical studies.

Problem Statement

Advanced pharmaceutical instruments like HPLC, NMR, and Mass Spectrometers are incredibly expensive and require specialized maintenance. Consequently, many educational institutions have limited units, restricting hands-on access for students. This often leaves students with purely theoretical knowledge, unable to visualize the operational workflow of these critical tools.

Our Objective

The primary objective of SimuPharma is to democratize learning by providing a free, accessible, and interactive virtual platform. We aim to:

  • Provide clear, concise information on the principles and workings of major pharmaceutical instruments.
  • Offer visual aids like diagrams, animations, and videos to enhance understanding.
  • Create simple, web-based simulators that mimic the basic operations of these machines.
  • Reinforce learning through interactive quizzes and assessments.

Scope & Future Expansion

Currently, the platform focuses on foundational instruments like HPLC, FTIR, and Gas Chromatography. Our future plans include:

  • Adding more instruments such as NMR, Mass Spectrometry, and Dissolution Apparatus.
  • Developing more complex and detailed simulations with variable parameters.
  • Creating a "Virtual Experiment" module where students can analyze sample data.
  • Translating content into multiple languages to reach a global audience.
